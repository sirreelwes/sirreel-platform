Rental Policies

Cancelation Policy

If notice of cancelation is provided less than 24hrs from the date of pickup, you agree to pay (1) daily rate for all equipment and vehicles booked. Whenever possible, SirReel will re-rent the canceled equipment and vehicle(s) and waive the cancelation fee for those item(s). Notice of cancelation must be provided in writing via email to your SirReel account representative.

Missing Equipment Return Policy

Any equipment missing from an order return will be billed as a loss. Loss invoices are Net15 (due 15 days from receipt). Any missing equipment returned within the 15 day payment period will be removed (or refunded) from the invoice. Any equipment returned after the 15 day payment period will be issued as a credit, with the standard rental cost of the equipment deducted from the credit total based on the number of days from the final day of the 15 day payment period and the missing equipment return date.

Administrative Fee

SirReel applies a ten percent administrative fee to all loss, theft, or damage charges. This fee covers internal processing, documentation, coordination with third parties, and administrative labor related to the claim.

Discounts Policy

All discounts are on Quickpay terms and invoices must be paid via credit card within 5 days. 5 days after the final invoice is sent, the discounts will expire and will not be reinstated. If your project has negotiated a Quickpay discount, please make sure payment is made within 5 days to avoid forfeiting that discount.

Payment Terms

Long-term projects (where the first and last day of rental spans more than 14 days) require payment in full before the first day of rental. Clients with accounts that have existing payment terms require a 50% deposit prior to the first day for long-term projects. Rentals used for any type of 'event' must be paid in full before the first day of rental.
